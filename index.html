FAQ

Our tour packages are designed to be all-inclusive for your convenience. The price typically covers your accommodation in carefully selected hotels, airport transfers, and all internal transportation according to the itinerary. It also includes guided tours and activities mentioned in the program, the services of a professional tour guide, and daily breakfast (with other meals as specified). Please be aware that international airfare, travel insurance, visa fees, and personal expenses are not included unless explicitly stated.

We understand that plans can change. Our standard policy offers a full refund for cancellations made more than 60 days before the departure date. For cancellations made between 30 and 60 days before departure, a 50% refund will be issued. Unfortunately, we cannot provide refunds for cancellations made less than 30 days before departure. We highly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance to cover any unforeseen circumstances.

While we strongly recommend travel insurance for your safety and peace of mind, it is not mandatory for all our tours. However, for certain adventure trips, it may be a requirement. We offer comprehensive insurance plans that cover trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and lost luggage. Please contact our agents for more details.

Our team closely monitors all flights and itineraries. In the event of a delay or disruption, we will assist you in re-booking connecting flights and accommodations wherever possible. This is another reason why we emphasize the importance of travel insurance, as it can help cover additional costs incurred due to such delays.
